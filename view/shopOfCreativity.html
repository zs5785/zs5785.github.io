About this Project

You play as a young wizard apprentice who dreams of becoming a Supreme Grand Master in wizardry. However, to take the exam, you need coins, which is why you decide to rent a shop to sell exotic items.

As a wizard, you can create spells and customize them using spell components. There are six types of spells, each with unique characteristics. Additionally, your spell can trigger the next spell if there's a trigger component on your spell.

Since you have rented the shop, it's important to note that you need to pay rent every week. Failure to do so will result in Game over. To assist you in selling items, you have the option to modify your shop by adding different types of furniture. Exotic items can be acquired by either venturing into a dungeon to find them or by crafting them yourself.
