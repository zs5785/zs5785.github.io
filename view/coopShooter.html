About this Project

I've never created a multiplayer game before and want to learn Unreal. That's why I'm developing this game. The game involves defending a base with your friends against waves of enemies.

Enemies drop scrap that can be used to purchase weapons or weapon modifications, which come in three levels. And Level 3 Weapon Mod has additional effects. You can combine two mods of the same level to upgrade its level.

You can customize your weapon to fit your playstyle, whether it's supporting your teammates by debuffing enemies or empowering your weapon.
