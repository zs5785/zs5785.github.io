About this Project

In this game, the player plays as a robot whose objective is to seek vengeance and eliminate anyone who stands in its way. You can carry up to four guns, which can be upgraded by adding various modifications to them. With 86 weapon modifications available, players can customize their guns to fit their playstyle. Additionally, the guns are randomly generated with different weapon parts, providing a unique experience for each gun found.

With Wave Function Collapse Level Generation and over 40 unique enemies, including bosses, this game offers endless replayability through randomly generated levels and challenging foes.
